Who We Are

Guardians of India's Sacred Heritage

Maa Baglamukhi Peeth Parishad is a premier institution dedicated to the preservation, research, and dissemination of India's profound spiritual and mystical sciences. Founded under the divine grace of Maa Baglamukhi — the tenth Mahavidya — our Parishad is a living tradition of knowledge.

Our scholars, Pandits and Acharyas are devoted practitioners with deep academic grounding, offering authentic guidance in Jyotish, Tantra Siddhi, Mantra Shastra, Yantra Vidya, and Ratna Shastra (Gemstones) to thousands of devotees and seekers.

Jyotish Vidya — Vedic Astrology

The eye of the Vedas, Jyotish is India's ancient science of light and time. Our research covers classical texts including Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra, Saravali, Phaladeepika, and Brihat Jataka, encompassing natal astrology, Prashna (horary), Mundane astrology and Nadi traditions.

🔥
Tantra Shastra

Tantra is a vast spiritual technology for working with cosmic energies. Our Parishad specialises in the Shakta Tantras — particularly Baglamukhi, Kali, Tara, Chhinnamasta and Dhumavati traditions — preserving their authentic lineage and ritual protocols.

Shakta Tantra Shatkarma Kavach Siddhi
🙏
Mantra Shastra

Mantra is sacred sound vibration — the divine in audible form. Our scholars study and teach the phonetics, metre, deity correspondence and energetic protocols of Vedic, Tantric and Puranic mantras, including Bija Mantras, Stotra compositions and Mala Mantra traditions.

Bija Mantra Deeksha Japa Vidhi Stotra
✡️💍
Yantra & Ratna Vidya

Yantras are sacred geometric expressions of divine consciousness. Ratna Shastra is the science of planetary gemstones. Both function as receivers and transmitters of cosmic frequencies. Our experts study the Yantra construction, consecration and the correlation of gems to planetary forces.

Shri Yantra Prana Pratishtha Navaratna Uparatna
